Vault of Glass

Encounters

Encounter #1: Waking Ruins

To access the Vault, players must activate three Vex plates outside its front door, and keep them activated continuously for five minutes.
This builds a spire in the middle of the area, which unlocks the gate.

Encounter #2: Templar

As you approch the Templar's area you will see a Relic shield called The Aegis in the center of the room.
Upon picking this up you will start the first boss encounter of this raid. The Templar is surrounded in a white shield that prevents it from being damaged, except by the Relicâ€™s Super ability.
When hit by the super the shield will be broken allowing damage until the Templar is dead.

Encounter #3: Atheon

This is the final boss encounter in the raid. Early in the fight, Atheon teleports three of your teammates to either Mars or Venus.
There on either Mars or Venus you will see the same Relic from the Templar encounter. Once again you must build up a super on the Relic.
Then the three teammates still in the boss room must shot Atheons side depending on if they are on Mars or Venus.
Then the person with the Relic will break Ateon's shield allowing other teammates to damage the boss until he is dead.

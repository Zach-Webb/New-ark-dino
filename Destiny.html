Destiny 2 Raids

What Is A Raid?

A Fireteam(of 6 people) embarks on a difficult quest to defeat a final boss,
and when it succeeds, the feeling of accomplishment is beyond comparison.

Destiny makes these raids with different mechanics involved in each one that sets it apart from the others.
Fireteams must learn and perform these mechanics in order to defeat a final boss at the end of the raid.

I have picked my two favorite raids to make an encounter guide for.
These website pages can help new players try to understand these raid mechanics
in order to succeed in thier next raid.
